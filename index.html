Our Story

The BigBite Story

BigBite is a local fast-food destination serving freshly prepared burgers, pizzas, shawarma, soups, wings, and family combo meals. Our mission is simple — deliver great taste, generous portions, and unbeatable value with every single order.

Located in the heart of Hangu at 1st Floor, Atiq Plaza on Station Road, we're your neighbourhood fast food haven. Whether you're craving a solo meal, feeding the family, or treating your friends — BigBite has you covered.
